  • Project 6



    My game concept is tied to my personal perception that people are an amalgamation of diverse cultures, experiences, and choices. In this game, players cultivate seeds into distinct plants by selecting different elements. The plants' growth and final form vary depending on the elements chosen, leading to a variety of patterns and colors on the plants. The game aims to let players understand that everyone is unique through the pursuit of different endings, reflecting the individuality of each person in the world and also showing the different possibilities that people can have.
